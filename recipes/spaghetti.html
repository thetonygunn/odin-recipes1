Dad's Spaghetti

A simple spaghetti recipe that you can make in 25 minutes that can serve up to 6 people

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, minced
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed tomato soup
  • 2.5 cups water

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef with onion until meat is brown. Drain, then return mixture to pan with tomato paste
  3. Add meat sauce over cooked pasta
